Refund Policy


RETURNS
Our policy lasts 30 days. If 30 days have gone by since your purchase, unfortunately we can’t offer you a refund or exchange. To complete your return, we require a receipt or proof of purchase.


REFUNDS (if applicable)
We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If you are approved, then your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your LeapWired wallet within a certain amount of days.


LATE OR MISSING REFUNDS (if applicable)
If you haven’t received a refund yet, check your LeapWired wallet again. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted. If you’ve still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at [info@leapwired.com].
